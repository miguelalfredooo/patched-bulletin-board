## PROMPT MODIFIERS — QUICK REFERENCE

**For heavier, darker stone:**
`--no bright colors, no white backgrounds, no highlights`

**For more age/wear:**
`heavily weathered, centuries of erosion, ancient patina, surface worn`

**For cleaner documentation:**
`museum quality photography, white gloves handling, institutional lighting`

**For more cinematic sites:**
`35mm film grain, anamorphic lens, deep shadows, atmospheric haze`

**For sharper object detail:**
`macro photography, ultra sharp focus, f/8, studio strobe`

**If text or labels appear:**
Add `--no text, no labels, no inscriptions, no writing` (unless the piece calls for it)
