COMPOSITION PRINCIPLES APPLIED:

Visual weight balanced vertically:
• Opening (heavy) orients reader
• Sections 2-3 (light/medium) settle in
• Section 4 (heavy) builds momentum
• Section 5 (light) provides breath
• Section 6 (medium) continues build
• Section 7 (very heavy) climax
• Section 8 (sparse) descent begins
• Section 9 (medium) anchors page
• Section 10 (light) closes gently

No two consecutive sections same visual weight.
Negative space intentional. Size varies throughout.
Eye guided downward through rhythm.
