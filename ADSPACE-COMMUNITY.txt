## Usage in Issues

Insert after section 6 (Photography) or before section 11 (Visual & Brand).
Replaces one standard section or appears as standalone module.

**Format compatibility:** A (VE), B (A), C (C), D (D), E (E), F (A), G (A)

**Tone:** Invitation, not demand. Casual, direct, welcoming.

**Line count:** 4–8 lines (keep under 15 to fit Telegram constraints)

---

## Metadata

- **Purpose:** Community contribution portal
- **Frequency:** Optional, can appear in any issue or once per month
- **Call-to-action:** URL (designbybulletin.com/submit or similar)
- **Audience:** Designers, makers, writers, visual thinkers
