COMPOSITION PRINCIPLES APPLIED:

Weight distribution creates vertical rhythm:
• Dense opening (full frame) establishes presence
• Sparse breath prevents eye fatigue
• Medium build (geometric) continues flow
• Heavy expansion (multiple clusters) builds momentum
• Light reset (sparse echo) provides pause
• Medium rise (nested) secondary building
• Very heavy climax (dense frame, multiple elements)
• Sparse descent guides downward
• Medium anchor grounds the page
• Light close finishes gently

No consecutive sections same visual weight.
Negative space used intentionally throughout.
Eye guided by size and density variation.
