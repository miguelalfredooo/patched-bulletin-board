# Module: Archives

**the-magazine**

**Version:** 2.0.0

---

## Purpose

Documents, publications, records, and ephemera — printed and digital materials that document culture, knowledge, and community. Archives are how cultures record and transmit what they value.

---

## What It Serves

- **Scene** — zines, flyers, posters, underground publications, community records
- **Practice** — manuals, instruction materials, teaching documents, how-to guides
- **Sound** — music publications, liner notes, music journalism, scene documentation
- **Table** — cookbooks, recipes, food writing, menus, food documentation
- **Space** — architectural drawings, interior design documents, spatial records

---

## Visual Territory

- Zines and self-published materials
- Concert flyers, event posters, promotional materials
- Underground or alternative publications
- Instruction manuals and how-to guides
- Cookbooks and recipe collections
- Music magazines and liner notes
- Newspapers and periodicals
- Architectural and design drawings
- Maps, charts, and informational graphics
- Business records, pricing, documentation
- Handwritten notes, letters, documents
- Magazines and editorial layouts

---

## Always

- Specific publication and context — what is this, when, from where
- Content visible and legible — the document itself is the subject
- Historical or cultural ground — why does this document matter
- Materiality evident — paper quality, printing method, condition
- Information value — the document communicates something about its culture

---

## Never

- Documents chosen purely for aesthetic reasons
- Loss of readability or legibility
- Decontextualized archives without cultural ground
- Generic "vintage publication" without specific reference
