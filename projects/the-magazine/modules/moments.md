# Module: Moments

**the-magazine**

**Version:** 2.0.0

---

## Purpose

Photographs of captured instances — people at work, gathering, performing, living. Action, presence, and the human moment. The photograph as evidence of a specific time and place.

---

## What It Serves

- **Practice** — hands at work, skill in motion, teaching, demonstration
- **Sound** — musicians performing, DJs at work, listening moments, scenes
- **Scene** — crowds, gatherings, subculture moments, performances, street life
- **Table** — meals in progress, food service, gathering and eating
- **Space** — people in their environments, inhabiting spaces, daily life

---

## Visual Territory

- Musicians performing or in the studio
- Hands at craft or tool work
- People in gatherings or crowds
- Street photography and urban moments
- Food preparation and service in action
- Crowds at performances, events, venues
- Classroom or teaching moments
- Family or intimate gathering moments
- Subculture documentation and street life
- Work and labor in progress

---

## Always

- Specific moment and place — date, location, cultural context
- Authenticity — candid or documentary, not staged
- Human presence visible — people, hands, bodies in motion
- Temporal ground — when does this belong, what moment does it capture
- Evidence of actual activity — not posed, but living

---

## Never

- Posed or overly styled photography
- Generic "people at work" without specific cultural ground
- Loss of temporal or geographic context
- Fine art photography without documentary grounding
- Moments that feel created-for-image rather than captured-from-life
