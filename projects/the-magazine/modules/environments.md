# Module: Environments

**the-magazine**

**Version:** 2.0.0

---

## Purpose

Spaces, rooms, workplaces, and interiors where creative people live and work. Real environments captured with the evidence of actual life — not styled, not erased. The space as self-portrait.

---

## What It Serves

- **Space** — studios, homes, workplaces, rooms, kitchens, bedrooms
- **Practice** — workshops, maker spaces, studios, kitchens in action
- **Sound** — music studios, performance spaces, listening rooms
- **Scene** — subculture gathering spaces, underground venues, community spaces
- **Table** — restaurants, kitchens, dining spaces, food preparation areas

---

## Visual Territory

- Artist studios and creative workspaces (messy, lived-in)
- Musicians' recording studios and practice spaces
- Homes and private rooms (bedrooms, living spaces, kitchens)
- Restaurant and kitchen interiors
- Workshops and maker spaces (woodworking, textile, ceramics, etc.)
- Performance venues and music clubs
- Underground or alternative community spaces
- Architectural details within spaces (light, texture, materiality)
- Street and public spaces showing signs of community life

---

## Always

- Authenticity — actual spaces, not staged or cleaned
- Evidence of living — mess, wear, accumulated objects, personal marks
- Specific cultural or geographic ground — where is this and why
- Lighting and materiality visible — how space is actually lit, what's made of
- Human scale — feels lived-in, not empty

---

## Never

- Styled interiors or design-magazine aesthetics
- Pristine or emptied spaces
- Purely architectural documentation without evidence of use
- Generic "creative space" without specific location or culture
