# Module: Art History
## Design by Bulletin

**Purpose:** Something from the past that matters today. Tell us what happened and why we care now.

**Sources:** JSTOR Daily, Artsy editorial, museum publications

**Always:** A specific event, work, or figure. Connect it to now in one move.

**Never:** Wikipedia summary tone. Dates without context.

**ASCII format:** B — Geometric Frame or A — Classic Object

**Version:** 1.0.0
