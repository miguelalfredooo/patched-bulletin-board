# Module: Painting
## Design by Bulletin

**Purpose:** A specific painting or painter in the news — a show, a sale, a rediscovery, a new body of work.

**Sources:** Booooooom, Artsy, It's Nice That

**Always:** Name the painter and the specific work or moment. Warm register.

**Never:** Art history lecture tone. "The artist explores the human condition."

**ASCII format:** A — Classic Object or D — Two Column

**Version:** 1.0.0
