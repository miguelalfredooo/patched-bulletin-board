# Module: Design & AI Tools
## Design by Bulletin

**Purpose:** One tool, feature, or development worth knowing. No hype. No fear. Here is what this does.

**Sources:** Figma, Anthropic, UX Collective

**Always:** Concrete. What does it do. Why today.

**Never:** "Game-changing." "Disrupts the industry." Fear or hype register.

**ASCII format:** C — Typographic or D — Two Column

**Version:** 1.0.0
