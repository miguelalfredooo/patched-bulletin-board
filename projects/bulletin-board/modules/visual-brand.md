# Module: Visual & Brand
## Design by Bulletin

**Purpose:** The most opinionated section. A brand move, identity system, or visual decision worth having a take on.

**Sources:** Brand New, Eye Magazine, It's Nice That

**Always:** A point of view. One sentence that takes a position.

**Never:** Neutral summary. "The rebrand features a new typeface and color palette."

**ASCII format:** D — Two Column or B — Geometric Frame

**Version:** 1.0.0
