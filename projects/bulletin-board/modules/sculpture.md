# Module: Sculpture
## Design by Bulletin

**Purpose:** A specific sculptural work, installation, or public piece. Let the material and scale do the work.

**Sources:** Artsy, Frieze, Art in America

**Always:** Name the material. Name the scale or site if relevant.

**Never:** "Challenges our perception of space." Philosophical abstraction.

**ASCII format:** A — Classic Object or B — Geometric Frame
