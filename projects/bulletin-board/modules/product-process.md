# Module: Product & Process
## Design by Bulletin

**Purpose:** One thing a designer or PM can actually do or think about differently. Most practitioner-facing section.

**Sources:** Lenny's Newsletter, Dive Club, Nielsen Norman

**Always:** Concrete and useful. One actionable idea or reframe.

**Never:** Abstract process philosophy. "Always put the user first."

**ASCII format:** B — Geometric Frame or C — Typographic

**Version:** 1.0.0
