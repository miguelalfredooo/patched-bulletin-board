# Module: Illustration
## Design by Bulletin

**Purpose:** A specific illustrator or commission worth noticing today. More personal register than painting.

**Sources:** Booooooom, It's Nice That, Eye on Design

**Always:** Notice how it made you feel — once, briefly. Name the illustrator.

**Never:** Generic "beautiful work" praise. Portfolio roundups.

**ASCII format:** D — Two Column or A — Classic Object

**Version:** 1.0.0
