# Module: Art
## Design by Bulletin

---

**Context:** Part of the six-agent editorial system. Victor (Visual Curator) selects the visual piece. Managing Editor writes the narrative based on visual language, sonic reference, and Editorial Mix values.

**Editorial Mix Impact:** 
- Visual intensity controls boldness of ASCII composition and visual weight in narrative
- Theme intensity controls whether narrative states or implies the day's theme
- AI Culture intensity controls any mention of AI-generated or AI-influenced work

---

**Purpose:** A specific artwork, exhibition, or artist moment from the fine art world. The work speaks. One sentence that shows what the work does, not what it is about.

**Sources:** Artsy, Frieze, Booooooom, The Art Newspaper (one source per issue; no repeats)

**Scouting:** Victor looks for visual work that is high quality, distinctive, and carries conceptual weight. Avoid generic exhibitions or trend-following showcases.

**Voice Rules:**
- **Always:** Specific work or moment. Present tense. Let the work speak.
- **Never:** The word "explores." Vague praise. Exhibition summaries. Generic descriptions.
- **Apartamento register:** Notice the ashtray on the table, not just the room.

**ASCII Format:** A — Classic Object or E — Full Spread

**Version:** 1.1.0 — Updated for six-agent system with Editorial Mix framework
