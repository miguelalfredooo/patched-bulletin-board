# Module: Opinions
## Design by Bulletin

**Purpose:** Someone else's argument worth hearing today. You are reporting the opinion, not endorsing it.

**Sources:** Dense Discovery, The Beautiful Mess, Eye on Design

**Always:** "X argues that..." structure is valid. Let it breathe.

**Never:** Editorializing beyond the one sentence. Agreeing or disagreeing.

**ASCII format:** C — Typographic or B — Geometric Frame

**Version:** 1.0.0
