Masthead Asset — Design By Bulletin™
=====================================

This is the official masthead structure for all covers. Use AFTER the 6-line DBB logo.

**Masthead Template (5 lines, fits in 34-char width):**

Design By Bulletin™               
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
Issue [NUMBER] · [THEME]          
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
                                  

**Usage in Covers:**
1. Place 6-line DBB logo at top
2. Leave blank line
3. Place masthead (5 lines above)
4. Then theme visual below

**Character Width:**
All masthead lines: 34 characters each (already padded)

**Fill in:**
- [NUMBER]: Issue number (e.g., 024, 025)
- [THEME]: Theme name (e.g., Icons, Memory)

**Example (Issue 024):**

Design By Bulletin™               
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
Issue 024 · Icons                 
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━  
                                  

**Do NOT modify:**
- "Design By Bulletin™" text
- Divider lines (━ character)
- Spacing/padding (all 34 chars)

**Required in all covers.**
