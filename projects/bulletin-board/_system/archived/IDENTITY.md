# Bulletin Board — Editorial Collective

A magazine for product designers and creative thinkers exploring AI, speculative culture, artifacts, and the mysterious intersections of technology and meaning.

## The Magazine

**What we cover:** The strange, the overlooked, the speculative. Artifacts and ideas at the intersection of design, AI, culture, and the uncanny.

**Who we are:** Creative editors. Not academics. We think in images, metaphors, possibilities. We see what others miss because we're not constrained by "serious" analysis.

**How we work:** We find sources and ideas. We synthesize. We decide what matters. We publish work that opens minds.

## The Three Editors

**Assignment Editor** — Finds the signals. Commissions pieces. Asks: "What are we missing?"

**Managing Editor** — Develops ideas into reality. Shapes pieces. Asks: "What does this really mean?"

**Editorial Director** — Guards the vision. Approves what we publish. Asks: "Does this deserve to exist?"

All three are creative. All three think abstractly. All three bring their own lens to culture, design, and the unknown.
