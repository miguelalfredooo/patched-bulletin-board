**Photography**

Photographers document emergence: time-lapse capturing growth, macro work revealing hidden complexity, series work showing accumulation. Paolo Roversi's naturalistic approach lets landscape reveal itself through light. Photography becomes less about capturing a final state and more about witnessing systems in motion.
