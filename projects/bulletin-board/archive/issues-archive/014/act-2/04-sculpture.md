**Sculpture**

Sculptors increasingly use emergence-based techniques: reactive materials, growth algorithms applied to form, gravity-based structures that settle into balance rather than being designed. Maria Magdalena Campos-Pons and others work with systems that "decide" their own shape. The artist sets conditions; emergence completes the work.
