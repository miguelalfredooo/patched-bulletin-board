**Painting**

Contemporary painters are deliberately designing chaos—layered marks, drips, collisions that resolve into unexpected coherence. Cecily Brown and Julie Mehretu exemplify this: dense surfaces where no single line dominates, yet unified composition emerges from apparent disorder. Emergence through accumulation.
