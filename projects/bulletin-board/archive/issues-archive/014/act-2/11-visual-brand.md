**Visual & Brand**

Visual identity systems increasingly embrace emergence: dynamic logos that recombine elements, palettes that shift based on context, typography that responds to content length. Brands like Google and Mastercard now use variable systems that produce coherence without rigid repetition. Identity emerges from rules rather than fixed assets.
