## Reggaeton Gravity — Bad Bunny Won Without Compromise

Twenty billion streams. A Grammy for Album of the Year—the first for a Spanish-language release in that category. But the album didn't compromise. "Debí Tirar Más Fotos" deepened reggaeton, rooted it, made it *more* itself. Reggaeton + trap + plena + dembow. Diaspora consciousness + nostalgia + Puerto Rican identity.

The question the music poses: When the world reaches for your sound, do you stay rooted or do you reach back? Bad Bunny's answer: you deepen. You add layers of what you know. You win by being more yourself, not less.
