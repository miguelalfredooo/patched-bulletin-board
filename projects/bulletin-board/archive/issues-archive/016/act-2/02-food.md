## Food as Philosophy — Convivencia as Export

Puerto Rican cuisine is being recognized globally not as technique perfected, but as philosophy made visible. Eight James Beard Award semifinalists in 2025—a record-breaking moment for the island. What's being exported isn't recipes. It's convivencia—the philosophy of presence, relationship, feeding as love.

Puerto Rican chefs understand something the rest of the culinary world is hungry to learn: food is a site of presence. When you're at a table together, you're not optimized; you're connected. In a world that monetizes distraction, that's revolutionary.
