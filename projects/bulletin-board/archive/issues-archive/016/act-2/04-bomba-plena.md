## Bomba & Plena — Grandmother's Dance, Granddaughter's Grammy

Bomba and plena are not dying traditions rescued by contemporary music. They're living ones, traveling, shape-shifting, insisting on their relevance. When Bad Bunny's Grammy-winning album samples plena, it's not nostalgia. It's lineage-claiming. What does it mean to sample your grandmother's music in your Super Bowl performance? It means tradition isn't separate from innovation.

The dance the grandmother danced in the 1950s is the rhythm the grandson produces in 2025. The tradition doesn't die. It travels. It changes form. It stays alive because it stays rooted. That's what living tradition means.
