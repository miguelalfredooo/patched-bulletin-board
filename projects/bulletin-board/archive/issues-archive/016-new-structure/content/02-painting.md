**Painting**

Every UFO sighting is a mirror. The UFO isn't a fixed object—it's a screen onto which cultures project their deepest fears and desires. In the 1950s, we saw invasion. In the 1970s, when institutions collapsed, we saw witnesses watching us. Today, we see ambiguity. The shift in UFO morphology maps perfectly onto cultural anxiety.
