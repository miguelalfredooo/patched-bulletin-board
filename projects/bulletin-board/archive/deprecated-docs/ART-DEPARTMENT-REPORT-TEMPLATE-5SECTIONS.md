**How The Art Department delivers curated selections organized by 5 sections.**

## Key Points for 5-Section Curation

1. **Organize selections into 5 sections** (Visual Thinking, Cultural Context, Critical Thinking, Tools & Innovation, Systems & Application)
2. **Each section has 2-4 pieces** (total 14-16)
3. **Specify subsection** for each piece (so Assignment Editor knows where it maps)
4. **Write Why/Insight/Connection** for each piece (shows your thinking)
5. **Write editorial insight paragraph** that explains what the 5-section organization reveals
6. **Preserve narrative flow** through the 5 sections
