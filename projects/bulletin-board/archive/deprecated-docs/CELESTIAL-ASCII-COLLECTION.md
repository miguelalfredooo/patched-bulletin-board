## Character Reference

**Geometry:**
- ◇ (outline diamond)
- ◆ (filled diamond)
- ▲ (triangle up)
- ▼ (triangle down)
- ● (filled circle)

**Borders:**
- ╔╗╚╝ (thick corners)
- ═ ║ (thick lines)
- ┌┐└┘ ─ │ (regular lines)

**Cosmic:**
- ✦ (star)
- · (dot/star)
- █ (solid block)
- → ← ╱ ╲ (direction)
