## EDITORIAL STRATEGY
This issue explores how artists, designers, and thinkers approach the *unknowable*. Not skepticism vs. belief. But: **how do we make work when the subject refuses to resolve?**

Thematic angles:
- UFO sightings as visual phenomena (design, composition, light)
- Paranormal as absence (what we can't see, can't explain)
- AI/digital culture as the new "alien"
- Artists who work with uncertainty, documentation, ambiguity
- The uncanny in design and visual culture
- Conspiracy aesthetics (how do we visualize unverifiable things?)
