**Visual & Brand — The Modular Identity**

Sagmeister & Walsh designed a brand identity that evolves while staying itself. Core principles remain; everything else breathes. This is the future of branding: not a fixed logo but a *system* with laws. Monotype's variable fonts work the same way—one file, infinite expression, but governed by mathematical constraints. Brands licensed to change are brands that age. They don't calcify. The underlying structure is invisible but absolute; the surface adapts to time and context. This is how things endure: architecture that's rigid enough to hold meaning, flexible enough to stay alive.
