**Design & AI Tools — The Interface as Architecture**

Design systems—Figma's component libraries, IBM's Carbon, Shopify's Polaris—aren't limitations; they're scaffolds. They let thousands of designers make decisions in parallel without constant communication. The structure *frees* people. Now AI design tools enter the conversation: Do they scaffold creativity or replace it? The best are the first kind—Midjourney's token system forces precision in prompting; constraints that sharpen language. Bad tools remove structure entirely, leaving chaos. Tools are architecture. The right structure multiplies hands at work. The wrong one multiplies confusion.
