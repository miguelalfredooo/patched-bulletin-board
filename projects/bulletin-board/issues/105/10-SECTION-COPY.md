**Product & Process — The Framework That Ships**

The best product teams move fast *because* of structure, not despite it. Sprint cycles, decision frameworks, modular codebases, design systems—these aren't bureaucracy; they're what allows distributed teams to create coherence. A well-architected codebase lets engineers move independently. A clear decision framework lets teams say no to feature creep. Spotify's squad model organizes thousands of people through intentional structure. The paradox: rigid architecture enables rapid change. Without the skeleton, every addition feels ad hoc. With it, you can iterate fearlessly because you know what stays fixed.
