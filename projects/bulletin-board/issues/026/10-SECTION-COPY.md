# Emergence

The ending of Inception refuses resolution on purpose. The totem spins. Does it stop? Emergence in design works the same way: you build toward an outcome, but what the work becomes in the world is not entirely yours to control. Users bring their own contexts, their own readings, their own needs. The product emerges from the interaction between what you designed and who uses it. This is not a failure of precision — it's the nature of the medium. The designers who accept this create work with room in it. Work that accommodates. Design that can be found by the person who needs it.
