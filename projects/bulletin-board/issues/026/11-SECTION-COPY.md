# Awakening

Every project ends with a return — from the compressed, rule-bending world of the work back to the flat light of the ordinary. Shipping is the awakening. The design leaves your hands and enters a world that doesn't care about your rationale or the careful decisions you made at 2am. It just encounters it as a thing that works or doesn't. This is the hardest part: letting go of what the work could have been. Inception understood this — waking up requires releasing the dream. The best work arrives ready, built for the world it's going into, not the one it was born in.
