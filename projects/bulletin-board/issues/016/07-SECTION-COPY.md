## Art History — Lineage and Innovation

Tradition and innovation aren't opposing forces—they're the same thing seen from different angles. When Bad Bunny samples plena on a Grammy-winning album, he's not referencing the past. He's claiming it as present. He's saying: this is where I come from, and this is where I'm going.

The grandmothers who danced bomba in the 1950s are dancing through their grandsons in 2025. The tradition lives because it moves.
