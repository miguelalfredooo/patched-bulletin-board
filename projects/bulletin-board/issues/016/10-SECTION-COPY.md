## Product & Process — Home as Practice

Home isn't just a place anymore. It's a practice. It's the intentional choice to stay rooted while staying open. It's the knowledge that tradition and innovation aren't separated—they're the same conversation across time.

When you practice home, you protect its meaning. And when you protect its meaning, you make it possible to share it. That's how living tradition travels.
