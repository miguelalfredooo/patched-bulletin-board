## Fashion as Assertion — Originators, Not Influences

"Global fashion is recognizing these brands as **originators, not influences**." That single sentence contains everything. Puerto Rican design carries a specific consciousness: genderless, bold, unapologetic about color and movement. Octavio Aguilar starting design at 13. Ricanstruction merging luxury with street culture. Not fusions of separate things—assertions of one complete aesthetic.

When the world reaches for what they're making, it's reaching for diaspora consciousness worn on the body. That's not influence. That's origination.
