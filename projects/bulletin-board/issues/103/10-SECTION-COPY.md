**Scatter in Motion: Animation Across Planes**

Animation trapped in a single rectangle is limited. But scatter animation across the screen, layering different speeds and directions, creates depth. The motion feels natural because it mirrors how things actually move in three-dimensional space.

Parallax scrolling scatters content across depth. One layer moves fast, another slow, and suddenly flat space feels deep. Multiple animations scattered at different timing create rhythm. The scatter itself is what makes motion feel alive.

Contemporary motion design embraces scatter intentionally. Not everything moves together. Pieces scatter across the timeline, arriving at different moments. The result feels coordinated without being synchronized. Alive without being frenetic. The scatter is the sophistication.
