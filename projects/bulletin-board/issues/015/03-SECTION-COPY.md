**Illustration**

Sylvia Trotter Ewens's recent series *Echoes of Elsewhere* — shown on Booooooom — works through layered illustration that refuses clean resolution, each mark sitting on top of earlier ones rather than correcting them, so the finished image carries the whole sequence of its own making. It is illustration as sediment: not the final image but the evidence of every image it passed through to get there.
