**Photography**

Derek Beck shoots a single 35mm roll while walking a random route through a city, iPhone in his jacket pocket recording ambient audio simultaneously — fifty rolls over ten years, forty cities, hundreds of strangers — and the project's premise is about frequency: the image and the sound vibrate together, neither quite explaining the other, the gap between them the space where something true lives. He returned to New York this year, revisiting streets he first walked in 2016.
