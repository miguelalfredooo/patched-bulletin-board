**Painting**

Lubaina Himid is showing at the British Pavilion in Venice with *Predicting History: Testing Translation* — paintings that move between text and cloth, personal archive and civic record, asking how much gets lost when one language (visual, cultural, temporal) passes into another. Himid has been making work about what survives translation for four decades; she's still the one asking the question most precisely.
