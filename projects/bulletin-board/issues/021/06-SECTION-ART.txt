║  Hands, Clay, Memory                    ║

  Not mass-made.
  Not optimized.
  Not disposable.

  Hand-thrown by a potter
  who has made 10,000 bowls.
  Each one slightly different.
  Each one carries intention.

  This cup was held by grandparents.
  It sits on the table now,
  worn smooth by use,
  cracked and glued and still poured into.

  Objects that hold stories
  hold communities.

  We are made by what we make.
