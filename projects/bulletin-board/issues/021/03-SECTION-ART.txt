  Erving Goffman: Objects as Props

  The stage is set.
  We are all actors.
  Objects tell us our roles.

  The counter:     "wait"
  The round table: "equal"
  The couch:       "relax"
  The chair:       "listen"

  Objects write the script.
  We follow without thinking.
