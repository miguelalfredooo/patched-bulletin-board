  How do we know if a design works?

  Watch people use it together.

  Proximity sensors -> comfort
  Video analysis -> natural flow
  Thermal imaging -> warmth
  AI observation -> spontaneous joy

  The object reveals itself
  through how we move around it.

  Design is a hypothesis.
  Testing is proof.
