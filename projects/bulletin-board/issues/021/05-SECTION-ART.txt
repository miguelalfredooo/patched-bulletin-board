  Before: office, home, coffee shop
  After: blur of everywhere + nowhere

  New rules for gathering:
  - Make sitting easy (cushions, height)
  - Make silence safe (nooks, screens)
  - Make connection visual (sight lines)
  - Make leaving graceful (open edges)
  - Make staying warm (materials, light)

  Post-pandemic, we redesign
  how people choose to be together.

  The social object survives.
