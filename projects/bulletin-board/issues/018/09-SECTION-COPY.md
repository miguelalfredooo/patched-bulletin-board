Motion design is computational poetry. Animation tells stories without words. When we design for interaction, we're designing choreography—how does the interface move in response to human touch? This is where constraint becomes creative: a 200ms transition can feel alive or dead. A curve can feel natural or rigid. Designers of digital tools are designing how billions of people move through information. This responsibility requires intention, not just efficiency.
