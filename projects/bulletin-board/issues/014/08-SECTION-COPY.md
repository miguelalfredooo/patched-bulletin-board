**Opinions**

Emergence challenges traditional design authority. If complex behavior arises from simple rules, the designer's role shifts from command to orchestration. This is unsettling—we lose certainty about outcome. But it's also liberatory: complex, adaptive systems outperform rigid hierarchies. The question becomes: what conditions enable the right behavior to emerge?
