**Art History**

The Japanese concept of *wabi-sabi* — finding beauty in the imperfect, the impermanent, and the incomplete — entered Western design consciousness through Leonard Koren's 1994 essay and has been misapplied ever since, most badly by brands that sandblast "aged" finishes onto things that were made yesterday.
