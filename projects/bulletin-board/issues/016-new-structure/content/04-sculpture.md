**Sculpture**

The Arecibo Message isn't just astronomy—it's a confession of what we believe about ourselves. In 1974, we transmitted our DNA, our solar system, our bodies into space, arriving in 25,000 years if anyone listens. But here's what keeps astrophysicists awake: we're not really trying to talk to them. We're trying to be heard. We're inscribing our hopes into radio waves, and the universe's silence teaches us something we were always afraid to admit.
