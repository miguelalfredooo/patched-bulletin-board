**Opinions — Ideology Breaking Down**

Certainty wears fastest when exposed to reality. The architect convinced modular housing would solve poverty watches it fail. The designer certain that minimalism would clarify sees it calcify into sterility. The critic who declared a movement essential watches it become antique. Not everything erodes equally. Soft ideologies—held loosely, tested often—develop resilience through weathering. Hard certainties crack under minor stress. The hardest positions are the ones that erode most visibly. This isn't relativism. It's just honesty: conviction changes when tested. The opinion that survives erosion isn't the one that didn't bend. It's the one that bent without breaking.
