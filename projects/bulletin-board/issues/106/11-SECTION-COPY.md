**Visual & Brand — Identity Through Precision**

A brand isn't a logo. It's the grid underneath. It's the relationship between elements. It's the 2-point increment that repeats across every application. Pentagram's identity work doesn't feel strong because the symbol is bold—it feels strong because the system around it is so precisely considered that the brand proves itself through proportion. You don't see the grid. You see a company that knows what it is. Detail in branding isn't decoration. It's the substance. The more closely you look at a well-made brand system, the more clearly you see intention. That clarity is the brand.
