**Art — The Deliberate Impossibility**

When Agnes Martin drew her graphite lines by hand, she wasn't trying to be straight. She was trying to be human within machinery. The barely-visible tremor in each line is the point—it's her hand saying "I was here, I was trying, I failed infinitesimally." This is art using precision as philosophy. Not perfection. Precision shows intention. It says: I looked long enough to see what matters.
